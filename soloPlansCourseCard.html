Solo-K Retirement Plans

The Solo-K Plan is a powerful retirement savings vehicle for solopreneurs who want to take control of their financial future. With higher contribution limits and tax advantages

Individual Health Plan Offers

  • High Contribution Limits: Up to $66,000 annually (as of 2023), combining employee deferral and employer contributions.
  • Tax Benefits: Contributions are tax-deductible, and growth is tax-deferred.
  • Roth Option: Choose after-tax contributions for tax-free withdrawals in retirement.
  • Loan Provisions: Borrow up to 50% of your account balance (up to $50,000).

Why Choose Solo-K Plans?

Save significantly more than with traditional IRAs. Enjoy flexibility in contributions based on your earnings. Build a secure financial future while reducing your taxable income. Supplemental Insurance Plans EmployerEase partners with Aflac to provide solopreneurs with supplemental insurance options that bridge the gap in affordable benefits. These plans include:

  • Accident Insurance: Coverage for unexpected injuries and medical expenses.
  • Critical Illness Insurance: Financial protection for major illnesses like cancer or heart disease.

  • Disclaimer: Supplemental insurance plan availability and specifics vary by state.

Why Choose Supplemental Insurance?

  • Protect your income and savings from unforeseen events.
  • Gain peace of mind knowing you’re covered beyond your primary health plan.
  • Access affordable, customizable options tailored to your needs.
