Reasearching Your Business Name

  • Start with a Google Search:

    Look for existing businesses using the name to ensure uniqueness and avoid confusion.

  • Check Domain Availability:

    Use tools like Namecheap or GoDaddy to confirm the availability of a matching website domain.

  • Search State Business Registrations:

    Visit your Secretary of State's website to ensure the name isn’t already registered

  • Verify Trademarks:

    Use the USPTO Trademark Database to ensure the name isn’t trademarked.

  • Social Media Handles:

    Check platforms like Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn for available handles.

  • Consider Industry Specifics:

    Avoid names that are too generic or commonly used in your industry.

  • Conduct a Survey:

    Ask friends, family, or potential customers for feedback on the name’s appeal and clarity.

  • Think Long-Term:

    Ensure the name reflects your brand vision and can grow with your business.
