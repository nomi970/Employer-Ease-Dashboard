Square

Square for Retail provides an intuitive and flexible POS solution tailored for small and medium-sized retailers. Its tools enable streamlined inventory management, seamless transactions, and actionable analytics.

Shopify

Shopify POS is designed for retailers who operate both online and in physical stores. It synchronizes sales channels for a seamless omnichannel experience.

Lightspeed

Lightspeed Retail is an advanced POS system offering detailed inventory management, analytics, and multi-channel selling for growing retailers.

Quickbooks

QuickBooks Online is a comprehensive cloud-based accounting platform that simplifies financial management for retailers.

Wave Financial

Wave offers free accounting tools tailored for small retailers, making it an ideal solution for startups and budget-conscious businesses.

BigCommerce

BigCommerce provides a robust e-commerce platform for retailers looking to expand online, offering seamless omnichannel integration.

BambooHR

BambooHR is a people management platform ideal for retail businesses with growing teams, focusing on HR, payroll, and benefits.

Deputy

Deputy is a workforce management tool designed for retail businesses, offering scheduling, time tracking, and team communication.
