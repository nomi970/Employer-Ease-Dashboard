Marketing Strategy

Marketing Strategy & Marketing Plan

A marketing strategy outlines a company’s long-term approach to achieving its business goals by understanding customer needs and building a sustainable competitive advantage. It defines the overarching goals and direction for marketing efforts.

9. KPIs and Measurement

Marketing Plan

A marketing plan is a tactical document that translates the marketing strategy into specific actions, timelines, budgets, and metrics. It focuses on short-to-medium-term execution to achieve the strategic objectives.
